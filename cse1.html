English

English is a global language used for communication, literature, and various fields of study, serving as a common medium for people of diverse linguistic backgrounds.

Mathematics

Mathematics is the study of numbers, quantities, shapes, and patterns, using logical reasoning and abstract thinking to solve problems in various disciplines.

Engineering Drawing

Engineering drawing is a technical form of communication that uses standardized symbols and diagrams to represent the design, dimensions, and construction details of mechanical, electrical, and civil engineering projects.

C Language

C language is a general-purpose, procedural programming language known for its efficiency and control over system resources, widely used in system programming, embedded systems, and developing software applications.

Python

Python is a high-level, interpreted programming language known for its simplicity, readability, and versatility, making it popular for web development, data analysis, automation, and artificial intelligence.

Physics

Physics is the study of matter, energy, and the fundamental forces of nature, exploring how they interact and behave across space and time.

Environmental science

Environmental science is the interdisciplinary study of the natural world, focusing on the relationships between living organisms and their environment, as well as the impact of human activities on ecosystems and sustainability.

Fundamentals of digital electronics

Fundamentals of digital electronics focus on the principles of digital systems, including binary logic, circuits, and components used to process and store digital information, such as gates, flip-flops, and microprocessors.

Chemistry

Chemistry is the study of the composition, structure, properties, and changes of matter, focusing on the interactions between atoms and molecules.

Data structures

Data structures are organized ways to store, manage, and retrieve data efficiently, including common structures like arrays, linked lists, stacks, queues, trees, and graphs.
