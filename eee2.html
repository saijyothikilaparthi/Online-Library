DC Machines and Transformers

DC Machine and TransformerA DC machine is an electromechanical device that can convert mechanical energy into direct current (DC) electricity, or vice versa. A transformer is an electrical equipment that transfers electrical energy from one circuit to another without changing the frequency.,

Electrical measurements

Electrical measurements are used to monitor the operation of electrical equipment and determine why it's not working properly. They are based on the concept of an electric circuit, which is a closed loop of conductors that allows electric current to flow

Digital Electronics

Digital electronics is the study of circuits that process information in binary form, or as a series of 0s and 1s. It's a key part of designing electronic products, including home appliances, communications, and automotive systems

Induction and Synchronous Machines

Synchronous and induction motors are both AC motors that are widely used. The main difference between the two is that synchronous motors rotate at a constant speed, while induction motors require slipcode. These concepts are similar across many programming languages, including Python, C++, C, and Java
