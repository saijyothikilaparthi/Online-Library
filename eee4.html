Grid integration of renewable energy sources

Grid integration of renewable energy sources is the process of incorporating new renewable energy generators into an existing power system

Smart grid communication and cyber security

Smart grid communication:Uses advanced communication technologies to monitor, control, and analyze the grid in real time. This can improve the stability and efficiency of the grid, and reduce the risk of power outages

Utilization of electrical energy

Utilization of electrical energy is the process of converting electric power into useful work, light, heat, or a combination of these. It is the end result of the generation, transmission, and distribution of electric power

Energy management systems and SCADA

DCS/SCADA is often used for production operation monitoring and scheduling, mainly focusing on process control direction. The energy management system focuses on energy consumption monitoring and energy management and analysis, and is an important tool for a set of enterprise energy management.
