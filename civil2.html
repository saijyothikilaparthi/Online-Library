surveying and geomatics

Surveying is the science of measuring and mapping the Earth's surface to determine the positions of points and the distances and angles between them. Geometry is one of the tools used by surveyors to perform their work.

Fluid Mechanics

Fluid mechanics is the study of the behavior and properties of fluids, including liquids, gases, and plasmas, and the forces acting on them.

strength of materials

Strength of materials is an engineering discipline that studies how materials resist mechanical forces and deform when used. .

Engineering Workshop

An engineering workshop is a room, building, or company that provides the tools and space for the manufacture or repair of goods. It may also be called a machine shop. .
