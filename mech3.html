Mechanical 3rd Year shelf

Design of Machine Members

Machine design in general is concerned with development of power sources and functional mechanisms. They may concentrate on such areas as design of engines or turbines or the functional portions of automobiles, machine tools,automation equipment.

Metal Cutting & Machine Tools

A cutting tool or cutter is typically a hardened metal tool that is used to cut, shape,remove material from workpiece by means of machining tools as well as abrasive tools by way of shear deformation.The majority of these tools are designed exclusively for metals.

Fuels Combustion and Emission control

Fuel combustion is a chemical reaction that occurs when fuel is heated and reacts with oxygen to release energy. Emission control system means employed to limit the discharge of noxious gases from the internal-combustion engine.

Tribology

Tribology is the study of how surfaces in relative motion interact, and how friction, lubrication, and wear affect them. Tribology is defined as 'the study of friction, wear and lubrication, and design of bearings, science of interacting surfaces in relative motion'. 'tribo-' meaning rubbing and friction.

Industrial Robotics

Industrial robotics is the use of automated, programmable machines to perform industrial tasks, such as assembly, packing, and inspection. Industrial robots are used in a variety of settings, including factories and farms.

Advanced Materials

Advanced Materials is a weekly peer-reviewed scientific journal covering materials science. It includes communications, reviews, and feature articles on topics in chemistry, physics, nanotechnology, ceramics, metallurgy, and biomaterials.

Solid Waste Management

Solid waste management is the process of collecting, treating, and disposing of solid waste from various sources. It includes the collection, transportation, treatment, analysis, and disposal of waste.
